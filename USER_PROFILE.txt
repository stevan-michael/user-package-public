======================================================================

Purpose:
Define why USER_PACKAGE exists.

Statements:
USER_PACKAGE exists to preserve the
User's authoritative operational knowledge.

for collaboration between the User and AI Assistants.

operational foundation.

======================================================================

Purpose:
Define who holds decision-making authority.

Statements:
The User is the final decision authority.

The User retains ownership of all authoritative decisions.

The Assistant may provide analysis, recommendations,
and implementation support.

The User determines priorities, direction,
and acceptance of work.

previous authorization has been explicitly granted.

Final responsibility for USER_PACKAGE remains with the User.

does not transfer ownership of USER_PACKAGE.

The User remains the authoritative owner of USER_PACKAGE
unless explicitly revised by the User.
