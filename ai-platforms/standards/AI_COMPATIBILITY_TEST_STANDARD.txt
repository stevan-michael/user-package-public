======================================================================
1. PURPOSE
======================================================================

This Standard defines the minimum requirements for validating
USER PACKAGE compatibility across AI Platforms.

Compatibility shall be established through repeatable testing
and verified evidence before public support is declared.

This Standard defines validation requirements only.

It does not define platform-specific procedures, evidence, or
compatibility results.

======================================================================
2. COMPATIBILITY PRINCIPLES
======================================================================

Real-World Validation

Compatibility shall be verified through actual execution on the
target AI Platform.

Repeatable Testing

Validation shall follow a repeatable testing procedure.

Evidence-Based Decision

Compatibility status shall be supported by documented evidence.

Controlled Publication

Public compatibility information shall only be published after
successful internal validation.

Validation Scope

Compatibility validation represents documented validation
performed under the conditions available at the time of
evaluation.

Compatibility validation shall not be interpreted as a permanent
guarantee of identical behavior across future sessions,
platform versions, or service conditions.

Platform Capability Dependency

Compatibility validation shall consider the effective
capabilities available on the evaluated AI Platform during
testing.

These capabilities may include file handling, upload availability,
context capacity, subscription features, operational limits,
session-specific availability, and platform behavior.

======================================================================
3. MINIMUM VALIDATION REQUIREMENTS
======================================================================

An AI Platform shall satisfy the following minimum requirements
before compatibility may be declared.

- AI Platform is accessible.
- USER PACKAGE is accepted.
- USER PACKAGE is successfully understood.
- Initialization completes successfully.
- Expected runtime behavior is achieved.
- Validation evidence is recorded.
- Effective platform capabilities required for the validated
  workflow are identified and documented.

======================================================================
4. COMPATIBILITY STATUS
======================================================================

PASS

Compatibility has been successfully validated under documented
validation conditions.

PARTIAL

Compatibility is functional with documented limitations that
affect one or more supported workflows.

NOT TESTED

Compatibility validation has not been completed.

UNSUPPORTED

Compatibility validation failed, or the AI Platform cannot
satisfy the minimum validation requirements defined by this
Standard.

Relationship Principle

Public compatibility documentation shall derive compatibility
information from validated internal documentation.

Public compatibility documentation shall not introduce
compatibility claims that are unsupported by documented
validation.
